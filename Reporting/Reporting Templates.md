# Reporting Templates

## Overview

The Reporting Templates Framework establishes standardized reporting structures used throughout the Integrated Risk Management (IRM) Governance & Reporting Program.

The purpose of reporting templates is to:

- Improve reporting consistency
- Support governance oversight
- Enhance executive visibility
- Simplify audit preparation
- Standardize communication
- Improve decision-making

All governance reports should follow approved reporting formats.

---

# Report Types

The program supports four primary reporting categories:

1. Operational Reporting
2. Management Reporting
3. Executive Reporting
4. Board/Governance Committee Reporting

# Reporting Standards

All reports should:

- Use consistent metrics
- Use approved risk classifications
- Present accurate data
- Highlight material issues
- Support decision making
- Maintain audit traceability

---

# Report Retention Requirements

Reports should be retained according to:

- Regulatory requirements
- Legal obligations
- Internal retention policies

- NIST Cybersecurity Framework

# Summary

The Reporting Templates Framework establishes standardized reporting structures that support operational oversight, executive visibility, governance decision-making, and audit readiness across the Integrated Risk Management Program.
