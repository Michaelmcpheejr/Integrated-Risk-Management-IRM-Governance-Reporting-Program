# Integrated Risk Management (IRM) Governance & Reporting Program

## Overview

The Integrated Risk Management (IRM) Governance & Reporting Program is an enterprise-style governance initiative focused on designing centralized risk operations, issue management workflows, remediation tracking processes, and executive governance reporting structures aligned to modern IRM operating models.

This program models how organizations structure governance, risk, and compliance operations to improve:
- governance visibility
- remediation accountability
- audit readiness
- risk traceability
- executive decision-making
- enterprise risk oversight

The operating model is aligned to enterprise governance concepts commonly supported by platforms such as ServiceNow IRM.

---

# Program Objectives

The objective of this program is to design and document a centralized IRM governance framework capable of supporting:

- Enterprise risk lifecycle management
- Issue management and escalation workflows
- Remediation tracking and validation
- Executive governance reporting
- Control-to-risk traceability
- Audit findings management
- Policy exception governance
- Third-party risk oversight
- Residual risk review and acceptance processes

---

# Governance Operating Model

This program utilizes a hybrid IRM operating model combining:
- governance workflow orchestration
- enterprise risk lifecycle operations
- centralized relationship mapping
- layered governance reporting

The model is designed to improve coordination between:
- GRC teams
- Security operations
- Compliance functions
- Business stakeholders
- Executive leadership

---

# Governance Structure

| Function | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| GRC Team | Operates governance workflows, reporting, remediation coordination |
| Security Team | Validates technical findings and control effectiveness |
| Compliance / Legal | Advises on regulatory obligations and policy alignment |
| Business Owners | Own operational risks and remediation activities |
| Executive Leadership | Reviews governance reporting and approves risk acceptance |

# Risk Scoring Methodology

The program utilizes a weighted enterprise scoring methodology incorporating:
- likelihood
- business impact
- control maturity
- detectability
